### 1.3 — Multiplying & Dividing Integers

**What the packet teaches**
- Uses **inductive discovery**: the teacher leads students through "multiply 3's in sequence" (3×3=9, 3×2=6, 3×1=3, 3×0=0, 3×(−1)=?, 3×(−2)=?) to let them *notice* the pattern, then says "This leads to a pretty amazing discovery…"

**What the app has**
